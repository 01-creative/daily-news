🔥【高危·EDUSRC】CVE-2026-20896 — Gitea Docker 认证绕过漏洞（CVSS 9.8），已进入活跃扫描阶段

→ https://securityaffairs.com/194902/hacking/critical-gitea-docker-bug-under-active-exploitation-exposes-repositories-and-secrets.html
